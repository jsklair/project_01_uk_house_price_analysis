## Key Project 01 findings

- In 2025, the highest affordability pressure was concentrated in London and the South East.
- Kensington and Chelsea had the highest lower-quartile affordability ratio in the latest-year output, at 20.89.
- County Durham had the lowest ratio in the exported least-pressured output, at 3.43.
- London had the highest 2025 regional median lower-quartile affordability ratio at 12.74.
- The North East had the lowest regional median at 4.66.
- Tandridge recorded the largest five-year increase in the exported SQL and Python outputs, rising from 14.07 in 2020 to 15.40 in 2025.
