# Executive summary

This project analyses first-time buyer affordability across areas in England and Wales. It uses lower-quartile house price to lower-quartile workplace-based earnings ratios as the primary measure, because this better reflects the entry-level end of the housing market than a headline average or median-only view.

The analysis is designed to identify where affordability is most stretched, how the position varies by area, and where national averages may hide important local differences.

At completion, the project will provide a cleaned area-year dataset, SQL analysis, Python profiling and exploratory analysis, an Excel review workbook, a Power BI dashboard and a concise business-facing case study.
